##  What This Flow Means










**[ Your Data ]**  
Your normal readable files.

**VeraCrypt Encrypts It**  
VeraCrypt scrambles your files using strong encryption.

**[ Encrypted Volume  ]**  
Your data is now locked inside an encrypted container.

**Unlock with Password + Keyfile **  
You use your password (and optional keyfile) to unlock the container.

**[ Mounted Secure Drive 📁 ]**  
Once unlocked, VeraCrypt mounts it like a normal drive so you can use your files safely.

##  What This Flow Means (Simple Explanation)

**[ Your Data ]**  
This is your normal readable files — documents, photos, anything.

**VeraCrypt Encrypts It**  
VeraCrypt takes your readable files and scrambles them using strong encryption.

**[ Encrypted Volume 🔒 ]**  
Your data is now locked inside an encrypted container. No one can open it without the key.

**Unlock with Password + Keyfile 🔑**  
You use your password (and optional keyfile) to unlock the encrypted container.

**[ Mounted Secure Drive 📁 ]**  
Once unlocked, VeraCrypt mounts it like a normal drive so you can use your files safely.
